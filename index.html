Personalising

Designs can be custom made to you requirements - (Try using our design application) or you can get inspiration from some of our popular standard frames and our recently sold items.

All our frames are handmade to order, so please remember that it could take a little time to get your order just right.

Please also be aware that however hard we try, some words just don't link together! We can use some of our 'linking words' to try and create your piece, but unfortunately sometimes it just can't be done to fit in the frame :(

Please e-mail me if you have some specific idea's of things you'd like, and I will endeavour to build your request.

We have a range of decorations and backgrounds to be added to the frame if required, from pink teddy bears to champagne glasses!


Small Frames - these are best for one word designs, no longer than 5 or 6 letters, however it can be used for a short word down and one across.

Medium Frames - this size is best for a few short words linked together, no more than 7 letters across and 4/5 down

Large Frames - these are ideal for customising. They can accommodate several names or words both up and down with linking words.
